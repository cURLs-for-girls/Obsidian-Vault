Introducing Cisco Silicon One P200 
Key Specifications 
Device Overview 
. 4th Generation Cisco Silicon One Device 
· 512 × 100G SerDes + 2 × 100 Gbps PIE, 512-radix 
. 16 Slices, 3.2T Interface Groups with fabric speedup 
· 51.2T Standalone or 14.4T/19.2T/25.6T Line Card modes 
· 5nm FinFET process 
· Integrated line rate MACsec, IPSec capable 
· 1.0GHz - 1.2GHz core clock, up to 19.2 Bpps 
DP 
· Full complement of Ethernet MACs, from 10GbE to 800GbE (ETC and IEEE 802.3df) 
· P4 programmable 
· 144MB Fully Shared on-die Packet Buffer 
16GB on-chip HBM for Deep Packet Buffer and LPM expansion
