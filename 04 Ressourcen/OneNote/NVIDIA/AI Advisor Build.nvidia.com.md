Use Case Telco:

Unstructured customer service workload: Over 10,000 calls per day, high staffing needs, many repetitive standard inquiries → inefficient use of resources

Opaque customer communication: Agents operate without systematic analysis or context from previous interactions

Inconsistent service quality: Customers receive different answers depending on the agent; lack of standardized knowledge

High turnover → knowledge loss: New agents are rarely familiar with the full product and service portfolio

Missed cross-/upselling opportunities: No intelligent product personalization during customer interactions

Problem (Industry Use Case: Telco Customer Service & Sales):

This workload is typical for a telco contact center: high volume, repetitive, and reactive rather than proactive – making it an ideal candidate for automation and support through generative AI.
